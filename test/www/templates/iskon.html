Welcome to Iskon Temple!





Iskcon Temple is known for its activities of engagement involving ardent devotees of the Lord Krishna. The temple enrols members for life, arranges programs for Krishna consciousness, arranges public programs for awareness in community centres and does songs and recitals admiring and praying to Krishna in public processions. They also have art competitions for youngsters and children. There is a gold plated pillar with a flag, 17 metres high, and a kalash 8.5 metres high. Prasadam is distributed to all devotees for free.
